MsingiAI Tokenizers

A groundbreaking project focused on developing high-quality tokenizers specifically designed for African languages. Our tokenizers address unique challenges such as complex morphology, tonal systems, and linguistic variations.

Key Features

  • Language-specific tokenization optimized for individual African languages
  • Support for code-switching and multilingual text handling
  • Efficient processing of morphologically rich languages
