MsingiAI Tokenizers

Building Tokenization Tools for African Languages

A community-driven initiative to develop high-quality tokenizers for African languages, which are currently underserved in the world of natural language processing (NLP). Our tokenizers are specifically tailored to the linguistic structure and diversity of African languages.

Why This Matters

Africa has over 2,000 languages, many with unique features like complex morphology, tonal systems, and non-standardized orthography. Traditional tokenizers often fail to handle these characteristics effectively.

Key Features

  • • Language-Specific Tokenizers optimized for individual African languages
  • • Support for Code-Switching and multilingual text handling
  • • Preprocessing utilities for text normalization and analysis
  • • Open-source and extensible architecture
