Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your privacy while building Constitutional AI for Cultural Safety

Last updated: March 11, 2025

1. Introduction

At Msingi AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or participate in our research initiatives.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect:

  • Name and email address when you contact us
  • GitHub username when you contribute to our projects
  • Information you provide in research participation

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring websites

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our research and services
  • Communicate with you about updates
  • Analyze website usage patterns
  • Maintain security of our services

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to read them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@msingi.ai
